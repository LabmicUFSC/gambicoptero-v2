Introduction

Gambicoptero-V2 is a project intended to design and build a quadcopter with wireless distributed control, meeting the constraints of a hard real-time system.

It has being designed as a Final Project of the Embedded Systems Laboratory course at UFSC

The name Gambicoptero is a joke combining the Portuguese words gambiarra and coptero, respectively workaround and copter.
